# TileFit Privacy Policy

Effective July 13, 2026

TileFit respects your privacy. TileFit does not require an account, and the developer does not operate a server that stores your game data. TileFit uses third-party services for analytics and advertising as described below.

## Information stored by TileFit

Game progress, scores, virtual coin balances, and app settings may be stored locally on your device so TileFit can provide its features. This information is not uploaded to a TileFit account or TileFit-operated server.

## Analytics

TileFit uses Google Analytics for Firebase to understand how the app is used, measure audience and feature engagement, and improve existing or future features. Google Analytics for Firebase may automatically collect an app-instance identifier, app lifecycle and session events, interactions with the app, and device and app information. It may also derive approximate location from an IP address; it does not use Apple's Location Services for this purpose.

TileFit does not require an account, does not assign a Firebase Analytics user ID, and does not intentionally send names, email addresses, or other contact information to Google Analytics. Analytics information is associated with an app installation identifier and is processed by Google. TileFit disables advertising-personalization and advertising-user-data signals for Firebase Analytics and does not use analytics information to personalize ads.

## Advertising

TileFit uses Google Mobile Ads to provide banner and rewarded advertisements. TileFit requests only non-personalized ads, which are not selected using your past behavior across other companies' apps or websites. Google and its advertising partners may still collect or receive information such as your IP address and general location, device or app identifiers, app and advertisement interactions, and diagnostic or performance information. This information may be used for contextual ad delivery, frequency capping, aggregated ad reporting, fraud prevention, and advertising performance measurement.

## Third-party processing

Google processes analytics and advertising information under its own terms and privacy practices. For more information, review the [Google Privacy Policy](https://policies.google.com/privacy), [Google's information for partner apps](https://policies.google.com/technologies/partner-sites), and [Privacy and Security in Firebase](https://firebase.google.com/support/privacy).

## Advertising consent and tracking

Where required, TileFit uses Google's consent system to ask for or manage consent before requesting ads. When Google requires a privacy-options entry point, it appears in TileFit's Settings screen. TileFit does not request Apple's App Tracking Transparency permission and does not use the advertising identifier to track you across other companies' apps or websites. Basic analytics collection is separate from advertising consent.

## Children's privacy

TileFit does not knowingly collect personal information from children.

## Changes and contact

This policy may be updated as TileFit changes. Questions can be sent using the developer support contact listed on TileFit's App Store product page.
